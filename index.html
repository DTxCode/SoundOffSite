About

Need to send a link to everyone in your immediate location? Whether it's a conference, a business meeting, or a social gathering, Sound Off makes it easy for anyone to transmit a hyperlink through a recognizable medium - sound.

Sound Off is a free application created during the MakeBU Hackathon by Team Null. Made with blood, sweat, caffeine and a productive 24 hours.
